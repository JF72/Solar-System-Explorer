Fun Facts about Me

  • Unlike other planets, my name comes from the Germanic word for ground
  • I'm 150 million km from the Sun
  • I only have 1 moon, named Moon
  • Days here are approximately 24 hours long and years are about 365 days
  • A majority of the planet is covered in water, around 71%
  • I'm the only known planet to support life, therefore making trees rarer diamonds around the universe
  • The atmosphere is mostly comprised of nitrogen followed by oxygen, along with 1 percent of other elements
