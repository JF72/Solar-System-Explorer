Hello from Venus!


Fun Facts about Me

  • I was named after the Roman Goddess of love and beauty, Venus
  • A day here is longer than a year here, one day is 243 Earth days while one year is 225 Earth days
  • Often reffered to as Earth's Evil Twin due to my extreme inability to host life but similar size
  • My distance from the Sun is 108 million km
  • At my closest I'm 39.8 million km from Earth and at my furthest I'm 259.7 million km from Earth
  • My atmosphere is mostly made of carbon dioxide and my clouds are made of sulfuric acid
  • Although I'm not the closest to the Sun, I am the hottest due to my thick atmosphere
  • I spin backwards compared to other planets, and my clouds smell like rotten eggs
  • 7 rovers have landed on me but they've all quickly melted or got crushed by my heat
  • I have no moon
