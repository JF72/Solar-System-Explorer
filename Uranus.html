Hello from Uranus!


Fun Facts about Me

  • Unlike other planets, I was named after a Greek God, the God of the sky, Ouranos
  • A day here is 17 hours but a year here is 84 Earth years
  • I'm 3.08 billion km from the Sun
  • At my closest I'm 2.6 billion km from the Earth and at my furthest I'm 3.15 billion km from Earth
  • Unlike other planets, I rotate on my side
  • I'm a mix of a gas and ice giant since I'm mostly made of hot and dense icy material
  • I also have rings like the other gas giants
  • I have 27 moons, my largest are Oberon and Titania
  • I'm mostly made of hydrogen and helium
